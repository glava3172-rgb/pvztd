About the Game

PvZ Tower Defense is a Roblox tower defense game inspired by Plants vs Zombies. Build towers, defend lanes, and survive waves of zombies!

Towers

Peashooter

Basic tower that shoots peas. Cheap and reliable.

Sunflower

Generates sun energy to buy more towers.

Fire Peashooter

Shoots fire peas, deals extra damage.

Zombies

Regular Zombie

Slow, basic enemy. Appears in early waves.

Conehead Zombie

More health, requires more damage to defeat.

Buckethead Zombie

Very tough enemy, can take lots of hits.
