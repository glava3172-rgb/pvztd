About the Game

PvZ Tower Defense is a Roblox tower defense game inspired by Plants vs Zombies. Build towers, defend lanes, and survive waves of zombies!

Towers

Cactus

Defensive tower, shoots spikes.

Watermelon

Deals heavy splash damage.

Cocotank

High damage, shoots coconuts.

Lemon Pult

Throws lemons at zombies.

Sunflower

Generates sun energy for towers.

Strawberry

Medium damage, fast attack speed.

Carnivorous Plant

Bites zombies in melee range.

Mr Carrot

Long range, shoots carrots.

Witch Hazel

Magic attack that slows zombies.

Jack

Explosive pumpkin attack.

Citron

Heavy firepower with high range.

Fire Peashooter

Shoots fire peas, deals extra damage.

Zombies

Regular Zombie

Slow, basic enemy. Appears in early waves.

Conehead Zombie

More health, requires more damage to defeat.

Buckethead Zombie

Very tough enemy, can take lots of hits.
